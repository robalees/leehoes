Schedule

Google Maps provides up-to-date subway directions. This is especially helpful since train service can vary quite a bit on weekends. If you have any questions about how to get to the venue (or anywhere else), please don’t hesitate to ask!

We ask that all guests please complete an at-home rapid COVID test prior to heading to the venue. If you don’t have access to a test, please let us know and we are happy to help get you one. Thank you for helping us keep all of our guests safe and healthy!

Friday,
September 16

  • 9:00-11:00 pm
    Welcome drinks at The Bonnie

    Take an N or W train to the Astoria-Ditmars Boulevard station and The Bonnie is less than a block’s walk away on 23rd Avenue.

    Drinks and light appetizers will be provided. The dress code is casual.

    23rd Ave, Queens, NY 11105

Saturday,
September 17

  • 6:00 pm
    Arrive at venue: Housing Works Bookstore

    Housing Works Bookstore is centrally located in the Soho neighborhood of Manhattan.

    The dress code is cocktail attire.

    The nearest subway stations are the Broadway-Lafayette Street/Bleecker Street stops (for the B, D, F, M, and 6 trains) and the Prince Street stop (for the R & W lines, as well as the N on weekends).

    126 Crosby St, New York, NY 10012
  • 6:30 pm
    Ceremony begins

    We can’t wait to celebrate with you! We plan to have an unplugged ceremony - we’ll ask that you put your phones and cameras away for the duration of the ceremony.

    We promise to share the amazing photos our photographer takes after the big day and our reception will be fully plugged in!

  • 7:00 pm
    Cocktail hour
  • 8:00 pm
    Dinner, dancing, and drinks!

Sunday,
September 18

  • 12:00-2:00 pm
    Brunch at Pig Beach-Queens

    Take an N or W train to the 36th Avenue station and walk five short blocks east. Alternatively, take an R train to the Steinway Street station and Pig Beach is just a 5-minute walk away.

    Classic barbecue mains and sides as well as beverages will be available for purchase.

    35-37 36th St, Queens, NY 11106
